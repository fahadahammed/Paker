So, Cloud flare has a lot of options to provide. I personally and professionally been with them for a while. It has been a great journey. Recently, I was thinking about trying out their workers. I did today and it seems very very promising. The documentation was also good. __wrangler__ is a nice idea and implementation. I wanted to try the concept and end up with static one.

If you hit here https://ip.tech0.xyz you will see your ip address. Pretty simple. It is running via **workers**.
